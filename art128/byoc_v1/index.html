Instructions for writing smart goals

Write your smart goals in a notebook or piece of paper that you can look at often

specific

When writing a goal make it specific to not be specific could lead to unexpected outcomes. don't say I want more money because if you find two pennies you have success. say instead I want to make $50,000.

measurable

Your goal must be measurable else how would you know how close you are to obtaining your goal.

Attainable

Your goal must be Attainable. If there is no way for you to get your goal than make a new goal. for example a goal to fly to the moon unless you are in a position to do so already is likely not attainable.

Risk

Those who live their lives in fear will not likely to accomplish anything. what are you willing to risk to achive your goal? write down what you will risk to accomplish this goal.

time

set an end date for when you want your goal to be accomplished. a goal without an enddate is just a dream. give yourself a reasonable timeline to finish start by making 30 day goals and work your way up.
